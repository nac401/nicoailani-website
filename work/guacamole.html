GUAC-A-MOLE

Your salary just isn't cutting it so you have to start making guacamole at work to make ends meet. Use the office paper cutter to chop ingredients, toss aside any office supplies, and make sure to keep doing your job!

    notes from me

    GUAC is a coalescence of systems, simulations, and mechanics all trying to have a conversation about labor, class, and capitalism. Like you are a writer and WORK, GUAC is an attempt to craft an emergent and often personal narrative from the systems of the game, Bogost would probably call that procedural rhetoric.
