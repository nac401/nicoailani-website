Paul is Dead

Liv, the lead singer of a rock band, catches their band mate, "Paul", in their dressing room. The only problem is... that's not Paul.

notes from me

Another failed pitch for the Abaddon project and probably the pitch that embodied my favorite voice as a writer: surreal, horrifying, but cut with humor.

When we table read it, the two producers on what would eventually be known as the Abaddon project looked at me with abject horror as we read through the nightmare sequence. What can I say? I'm using all of the tools at my disposal to scare you... even the budget.
