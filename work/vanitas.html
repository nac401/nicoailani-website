Vanitas

Vanitas is a jazzy mystery game where you play a constantly evolving dice game with the strange passengers on a train to nowhere.

accomplishments

  • Rising Tide Competition Winner. We submitted a trailer to the Blue Ocean Rising Tide competition and placed 10th out of thousands of submissions.

    • notes from me

    Vanitas is the largest game I have worked on so far. I worked as the lead engineer and as a narrative designer along with 5 other extremely talented individuals. Over the course of 3 months of development we were able to put together a demo and trailer. While this project has been tabled for our team to work on our graduate thesis, we will likely return to the project in the coming years.
