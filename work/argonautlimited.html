The Heist of the Argonaut Limited

Play the part of a witless criminal, hired for a daring train heist where everything goes wrong. Made in the style of classic RPGs and text games; read, roll, and choose your way through this thrilling adventure with countless paths and dozens of unique endings.

accomplishments

  • Playdate Catalog Feature. Featured on Panic's Playdate Catalog, meaning the game had to fit their standards of quality and accessibility.
  • Best Narrative, PCA 2024. Won best narrative at the Playdate Community Awards, meaning the game was nominated by the community and then voted on favorably by a panel of judges.

    • notes from me

    Argonaut Limited is essentially the first game I ever made. Making it was quite a journey of discovery since it was my first time writing for an interactive medium. You can read the whole story on the dev blog that I wrote leading up to the release of the game.

    Though I was the 'sole developer' of the game, I paid an artist and a musician to make some amazing art and music, and I was continuously supported by an incredible community of Playdate developers without whom I would never have been able to finish the game.
