you are a writer

Become a modern day writer in this short form typing incremental game. Write words to make money, find new ways to write more words, and find new ways to make more money.

accomplishments

  • Warp Door Feature. After the Markov Update the game was featured on Warp Door. Warp Door curates a collection of strange and unique games and digital ephemera from all across the web. You can learn more about Warp Door here.
  • itch.io Analytics. On release you are a writer made it onto the "New and Popular" and "Top Rated" lists for incremental and typing games. The game continues to see engagement from the "Top Rated" list.

    • notes from me

    you are a writer was the start of my desire to enmesh narrative and systems design and also the start of my obsession with typing games (which would later continue with WORK). This project was also the first time I dabbled in text generation using a rudimentary Markov Chain generator that used a corpus of my own creation.
