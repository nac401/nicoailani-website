narrative designer

My work as a narrative designer is influenced by 5+ years of screenwriting experience, ~10 years of experience as a Game Master of various tabletop role-playing games, and most recently, roughly 3 years of creating narrative video games.

narrative systems

I have experience designing, engineering, and implementing narrative systems using Lua and C#.

These systems have interwoven gameplay mechanics with narrative elements, such as the Heist of the Argonaut Limited's economy of knowledge paired with classic TTRPG-style skill checks.

Argonaut Limited (pictured above), was built using Lua with the Playdate SDK to support a variety of character "voices" and textual expressions.

My approach to designing narrative systems is wholly influenced by the requirements of the game. For Argonaut Limited, it was all about working within the confines of the machine (the GPU of a calculator) and the confines of the chosen genre meaning that the game was largely text.
